Colour Paradigms and Accessibility

In a globalised world where people from all across the globe can access the internet, it is important to ensure coders understand the significance of certain colours in certain regions. In addition, accessibility should be prioritised by making smart colour choices.

Colour Paradigms

Across the world, different colours have different meanings.

Red

In Chinese culture, red symbolises luck and happiness, and is worn during New Year to ward off evil.
