Website Layout

When creating a website, it's important to ensure the layout is intuitive for users.

Left to Right (LTR) languages versus Right to Left (RTL) languages

Localisation, the adaptation of a product to suit a particular place, is something that is important. With the rise of globalisation comes a need to cater to larger audience. This means, there may be a need to create RTL websites that cater to users that are reading site content in RTL languages.

Intuitive Layout

People are used to vertical scrolling. Therefore, they're more likely to see content that requires a vertical scroll compared to a horizontal scroll.
